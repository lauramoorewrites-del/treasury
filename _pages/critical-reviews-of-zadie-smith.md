- James Wood, ["Human, All Too Inhuman,"](https://newrepublic.com/article/61361/human-inhuman) *The New Republic* (2000). Review of *White Teeth*. The essay that coined "hysterical realism."
- James Wood, ["Tell Me How Does It Feel?"](https://www.theguardian.com/books/2001/oct/06/fiction) *The Guardian* (2001). Post-9/11 essay extending the hysterical realism argument; Smith is one of several novelists discussed.
- Sarah L. Courteau, ["Riffs and Raptures,"](https://theamericanscholar.org/riffs-and-raptures/) *The American Scholar* (2009). Review of *Changing My Mind: Occasional Essays*. *"A tendency to riff, which can be distracting in her fiction, is an asset here, leading her to make the fruitful associations that are an essayist's stock in trade."*
