- Daniel Muñoz, ["Is Daniel Muñoz an agent of the death of philosophy itself?"](https://bigifftrue.substack.com/p/is-daniel-munoz-an-agent-of-the-death) *Big iff True* (2026). Muñoz responds to a critique accusing him of "logocide" for advocating clarity in philosophical writing. He distinguishes between obscurity caused by difficult *ideas* and obscurity caused by poor *expression*, and argues that truly understanding something means being able to explain it at multiple levels.
