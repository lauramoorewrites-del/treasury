## Full books

- Þórbergur Þórðarson, [*The Stones Speak*](https://bookshop.org/search?q=9789979332862) (*Steinarnir tala*, 1956–58), trans. Julian Meldon D'Arcy (Mál og menning, 2012). His childhood memoir of growing up at Hali in Suðursveit — the district that holds Staðarfjall/Papýlisfjall. Described as the first complete book of his to appear in English.

## Selections / partial translations

- Þórbergur Þórðarson, *In Search of My Beloved* (selected fragments from *Íslenzkur aðall*, 1938), trans. Kenneth G. Chapman (Twayne Publishers, 1967). An abridged selection, not the complete novel. Long out of print.

## Shorter pieces / excerpts

- Þórbergur Þórðarson, "When I Got Pregnant" — a short story / excerpt from *Bréf til Láru* (1924), trans. Julian Meldon D'Arcy. Circulated as a standalone translated fragment.

## Uncertain / possibly unpublished

- Þórbergur Þórðarson, *Ofvitinn* (1940–41), rendered variously as *The Eccentric* or *The Idiot Savant*. Wikipedia states it was translated into English by Einar Steinn Valgarðsson, but a published edition has not been confirmed — this may be an in-progress or unpublished translation.
